About Evolve

Built on service, growth, and transformation.

Evolve Insurance Agency exists to empower individuals and families through protection, education, and intentional legacy building.

Founder

Evolve Founder

Justina Lorico

Justina Lorico is the Founder of Evolve Insurance Agency, driven by a passion to help families and entrepreneurs build financial security and lasting legacies.

Before entering the financial services industry, Justina spent over ten years in the restaurant industry, where she developed a deep appreciation for serving people, building relationships, and leading with intention.

Through Evolve Insurance Agency, she is committed to empowering communities with financial education, mentoring future leaders in the industry, and helping individuals protect their income, grow their wealth, and transform their legacy.
