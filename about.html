Evolve Founder

Justina Lorico

Justina Lorico is the Founder of Evolve Insurance Agency, driven by a passion to help families and entrepreneurs build financial security and lasting legacies.

Through Evolve Insurance Agency, she is committed to empowering communities with financial education, mentoring future leaders, and helping individuals protect their income, grow their wealth, and transform their legacy.

Our Vision
Empowering individuals to evolve into the best version of themselves — personally, professionally, and financially — so they can create lasting impact and generational change.
Our Mission
At Evolve Insurance Agency, we educate and equip individuals and families through financial education and innovative protection strategies. Through trusted relationships and a commitment to excellence, we help our clients evolve financially and prepare for the future.
