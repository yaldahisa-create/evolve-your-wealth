About Evolve

Built on service, growth, and transformation.

Evolve exists to empower individuals and families through protection, education, and intentional legacy building.

Founder

Evolve Founder

Justina Lorico

Justina Lorico is the Founder of Evolve Insurance Agency, driven by a passion to help families and entrepreneurs build financial security and lasting legacies.

Through years of leadership, service, and dedication, she has remained committed to educating others, empowering communities, and helping individuals make intentional decisions for their future.

Evolve was built from a desire to create real transformation — not only financially, but personally and generationally — through clarity, service, and purpose-driven leadership.
