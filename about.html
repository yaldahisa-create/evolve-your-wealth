About Evolve

Built on service, growth, and transformation.

Evolve exists to empower individuals and families through protection, education, and intentional legacy building.

Founder

Evolve Founder

Justina Lorico

Justina Lorico is the founder of Evolve, driven by a passion to help families and entrepreneurs build financial confidence and lasting legacy.

Through years of leadership, service, and education, she has remained committed to helping others make intentional decisions for their future.

Evolve was built to create real transformation — not only financially, but personally and generationally — through clarity, service, and purpose.
